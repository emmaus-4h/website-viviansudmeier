Andres Reisinger

Born in Buenos Aires, Argentina in 1990 – Andrés Reisinger is an artist, director, product, interior and 3D graphic designer. His experience merged with contemporary culture make up his inter-disciplinary practice today; stemming from his last 12 years of experimental and commercial collaborations with interior and furniture designers, including Patricia Urquiola, Cassina, Studio Proba, Space10 and Ikea. And global brands including Apple, Microsoft, Nike, Samsung, Rimowa, Uniqlo, Bloomberg, and Verizon. He has been recognized as one of the Young Guns Art Directors Club by The One Club for Creativity New York. And he has been selected by Forbes as 30under30 Europe into Arts & Style.
